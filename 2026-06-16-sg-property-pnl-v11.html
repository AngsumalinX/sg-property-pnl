Dates & prices

Purchase costs ? BSD (Buyer's Stamp Duty) is calculated on the purchase price using tiered rates (1–6%). Legal and valuation fees are typical third-party costs.

Sale costs ? SSD (Seller's Stamp Duty) applies if you sell within 4 years of purchase (for properties bought from Jul 2025). Agent commission is typically 1–2% of sale price.
